Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about solar energy and our services.

The cost depends on your energy needs, roof size, and system type. Residential systems typically range from $5,000 to $30,000 before incentives. We offer free consultations and quotes to provide accurate pricing for your specific situation.

Most residential installations are completed in 1-3 days after permits are approved. The entire process from consultation to activation typically takes 4-8 weeks, depending on your location and utility company requirements.

Yes, we partner with several financial institutions to offer competitive solar loans with flexible terms. Many customers see immediate savings as their loan payments are often less than their previous electricity bills.

Solar systems require minimal maintenance. We recommend occasional cleaning of panels (especially in dusty areas) and annual inspections. Most systems include monitoring that alerts you to any performance issues.

Standard grid-tied systems shut off during outages for safety. With battery storage, you can have backup power. Our hybrid systems automatically switch to battery power during outages, keeping your essential circuits running.
